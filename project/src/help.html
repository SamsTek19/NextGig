Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify my account?

+

To verify your account, you'll need to provide a valid government ID and complete our verification process. This helps ensure a safe environment for all users.

What happens if I have a dispute with a client?

+

NextGig provides dispute resolution services. Contact our support team within 48 hours of the issue, and we'll help mediate the situation fairly.

How do I access the Skill Hub courses?

+

Skill Hub courses are available in your dashboard. Free courses are accessible immediately, while premium courses require a subscription or individual purchase.
